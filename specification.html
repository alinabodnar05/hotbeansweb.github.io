Job Specification

To join our team of web developers at Hot Beans Web you will need to provide an evidence of your background knowledge and training. Listed below are qualifications our team is looking for:

  • GCSE in IT (or BTEC equivalent)
  • GCSE in Art (or BTEC equivalent)
  • Level 2 award in Creating a Website
  • Level 2 Certificate in Interactive Media
  • Level 3 Diploma in IT User Skills
  • LeveLevel 2/3 Diploma in Creative iMedia
  • Level 3 Diploma in Information Technology (Specialist)

What we want in a candidate:

We are looking for an ambitious and talented web developer. To work at Hot Beans Web, you will need to have qualities like positive attitude, good teamwork, passion for coding and enthusiasm.

We also expect you to have a solid background of web developing skills, work directly with clients to understand their needs and requirements; be good at designing, developing, programming, testing, upgrading, and debugging web applications; collaborating with designers and other developers. Some previous working experience in this field would also be an asset.
